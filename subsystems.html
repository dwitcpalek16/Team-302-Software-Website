  • elevator

When the front banner sensor detects a tote, the elevator is raised. Once it reaches the top, the intake wheels are started. This starts to suck in the tote. Once the tote is detected by the rear banner sensor, the roller wheels stop and the elevator lowers, ready to lift the tote. This segment of robot function is completely autonomous.

When the operators want to score, they press a button and the rollers smoothly push the tote out
